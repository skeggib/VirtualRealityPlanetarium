# Planetary data

Columns:

- `planet`: planet's name
- `a`: major radius (AU)
- `lambda`: mean longitude at epoch (째)
- `e`: eccentricity
- `I`: inclination to ecliptic (째)
- `omega`: longitude of perihelion (째)
- `Omega`: longitude of ascending node (째)
- `T`: orbital period (years)
- `P`: day duration (Earth days)
